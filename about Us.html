About ZACMOTORS – Your Trusted Car Partner

We're passionate about making car ownership easy and transparent, delivering a hassle-free experience for all your car needs.

About Us

Since its incorporation in 1977, ZacMotors — the automobile arm of Coscharis Group — has been driven by the vision of remaining “timeless in relevance and value”. With their exceptional customer service, experienced team, and competitive prices, ZacMotors has become a go-to choice for those looking to buy a car in Nigeria. One of the key advantages of buying from ZacMotors is the flexibility.

Brand Portfolio

We represent a diverse mix of globally respected automobile brands, supporting everything from premium luxury to accessible value models.

For example, via our vehicle-assembly subsidiary, we began local production of the Ford Ranger in Nigeria in 2014/15.

What We Offer

lobal brand standard + local support.

We bring world-class automobile brands to Nigeria, while providing after-sales, genuine parts and service close to you.

Premium experience for discerning customers.

Our luxury portfolio (BMW, Jaguar Land Rover, INEOS Grenadier) is designed to meet the highest expectations in comfort, performance and style.

Accessible mobility solutions.

Through value-brand models and local assembly, we provide more affordable options to meet evolving market needs.

Achievements & Recognition

Our commitment to excellence has been recognised across the industry. For example, in 2024 we were awarded “Multi-Luxury Company of the Year” by the Nigerian Auto Journalists Association.

Looking Ahead

As we move forward, ZacMotors will continue to deepen its footprint across Nigeria and West Africa, further enhance its after-sales network, expand locally-assembled offerings and deliver sustainable mobility solutions for the future.
