About Us

Since its incorporation in 1977, ZacMotors — the automobile arm of Coscharis Group — has been driven by the vision of remaining “timeless in relevance and value”.

Brand Portfolio

We represent a diverse mix of globally respected automobile brands, supporting everything from premium luxury to accessible value models.

For example, via our vehicle-assembly subsidiary, we began local production of the Ford Ranger in Nigeria in 2014/15.
